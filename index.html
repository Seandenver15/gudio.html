Robotics Registration

Robotics is a multidisciplinary field encompassing mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, computer science, and artificial intelligence, focused on designing, constructing, operating, and applying robots. Robots are programmable machines capable of carrying out a complex series of actions automatically, ranging from simple repetitive tasks in manufacturing to sophisticated autonomous systems used in exploration, healthcare, and many other sectors. The core goal of robotics is to create machines that can assist or replace humans in performing tasks, often in environments or situations hazardous or unsuitable for human workers.
